Creation

AIESEC was introduced to Algeria via AIESEC Tunisia, by the MCP of AIESEC Tunisia, it was an expansion around 2009, and then it gained its full membership later on. We started as an association that was restricted into charitable activities and social impacting, having humble meetings of 10 to 12 members in Rouiba in a wedding space!

Evolution

AIESEC In Babez has made a big leap since its creation. By helping more than 2000 young algerians develop themselves and share their culture through volunteering, AIESEC In Babez has made an important impact in promoting the values of Diversity and World Citizenship into our society.
