About the Forum

YouthSpeak Forum is a premiere event that brings together both young and senior leaders to form a diverse cross-sector and multi-generational space to create solutions and actions to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals relevant in a specific community.

Every YouthSpeak Forum is designed to inspire the delegates through power talks done by exceptional keynote speakers. We want to open the minds of delegates to think differently and spark innovative ideas.

Our partners engage with delegates to give new perspectives on SDGs through case rounds and idea generation spaces. Youth, businesses and NGOs are able to exchange opinions and learn about solutions.

We provide a practical experience for delegates of co-creating solutions for SDGs with their peers and experts in the room. We connect resources and networks with the projects to enable a real impact to occur.
