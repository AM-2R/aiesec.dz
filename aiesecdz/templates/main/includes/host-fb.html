Dayafni “Host me”

AIESEC in Algeria is looking for you to host

and share your culture with our exchange participants.

Why host an exchange participant ?

Expand your international network.

Learn a new language and culture.

Share your tradition and the algerian hospitality.

Experience a crosscultural environment.

Share the global village with the exchange participant.

What are you expected to do ?

Provide a bed, a personal space for the exchange participant.

Provide one meal per day.

Provide a bathroom.

AIESEC will provide pick up and assistance.

The exchange participant will provide his own insurance.

Who will you host?

You will host exchange participants between 18 and 30 years old from different countries participating in programs of 6 to 8 weeks to act towards sustainable development goals and create an impact in the society, they will work from 8 am to 4 pm. The common language they all speak is english.

When will you host?

Our host program is available all the year, and the duration is between 6 to 8 weeks.

Who can host?

Every family interested in hosting a foreign exchange participant, the only requirement is a family with all the housing standards and utilities (bathroom, bed, one meal,...ect), open minded and ready to share their culture. AIESEC will give assistance to the exchange participant; the host will only provide the necessities in the house.

How can you be a host family?

1. Fill the form.

2. Fulfill the housing standards.

3. Specify your availability for hosting.

4. Wait for AIESEC to contact you.

5. Schedule an interview with AIESEC.

6. Wait for the answer.

7. Wait for the exchange participants to come.

8. Ready to host our exchange participant.
