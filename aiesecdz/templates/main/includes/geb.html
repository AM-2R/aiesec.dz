Frequently Asked Questions

Projects are available all year long. However, most of the internships run either in summer (June-August) or winter (December-February).
Global Entrepreneur is in itself a non-paid project in which accommodation is provided. Although this accommodation is the basis, some projects as well cover food, transportation or even give you a financial compensation.
In general we advise to start applying 2,5 month in advance. At this point in time most of the projects for the time that you would like to go are available and you have still time to look around and apply to several different projects.
It really depends on the opportunity which background is preferred. Most of the time it is very flexible, but you can have a look at the opportunity since sometimes skills are put as preferred whereas sometimes they are required.
The opportunities in the portal are from all over the world. However, the most opportunities can be found in Latin America, Europe and Asia Pacific.
